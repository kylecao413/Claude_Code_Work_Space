Installation of new sanitary and stormwater piping systems throughout the Main Building, East Wing, and West Wing of the Smithsonian National Museum of Natural History to replace existing end-of-service-life infrastructure. Work must protect the museum's collections, exhibits, ongoing research operations, and historic architectural features. Phased execution across multiple wings; includes below-slab sanitary, in-wall piping, pressure testing, and commissioning.
