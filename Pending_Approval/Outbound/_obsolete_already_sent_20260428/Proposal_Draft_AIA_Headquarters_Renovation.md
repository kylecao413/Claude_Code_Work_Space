## Project Description

Renewal of the AIA Headquarters — a Type 1B office building with one occupied level below grade and seven above grade (total 150,000 GSF; inspection scope covers Lower Level plus Levels 1-4 full renovation). Structural work is limited to: (1) rooftop MEP platform and photovoltaic panels on existing roof; (2) new atrium and stair openings at existing Level 1 & 4; (3) new feature stair from Lower Level to Level 1; (4) new feature stair from Level 3 to Level 4; (5) façade modernization and plaza-level landscape modifications. No new foundations (existing 1971 structural drawings govern). Upper floors (Levels 5-7) are tenant-occupied with limited scope (perimeter mechanical upgrades, core-restroom plumbing, and storm drain routing — no interior framing/close-in work on tenant floors). Full MEP and telecommunications system renewal; replacement of exterior glazing system on all levels including a new façade feature.
