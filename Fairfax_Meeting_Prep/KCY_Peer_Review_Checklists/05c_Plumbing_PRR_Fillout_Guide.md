## Fill-rules (recap)

| Mark | When | Comment |
|------|------|---------|
| ☑ Complies | Drawings show compliance | Blank or "OK" |
| ☐ N/A | Doesn't apply | One-line scope reason |
| ☐ + comment | Deficiency | "See Deficiency Log #X" |

## Section-by-section

**§A — General Building Information** — Mostly never N/A. Verify each line on plans.

**§B — County Ordinances** — Most lines N/A unless trigger:
- Pools → N/A unless pool in scope
- Backflow → almost always Complies (every commercial has backflow somewhere)
- Separate sewer/water → Complies if drawings show separate connections
- Car wash recycle → N/A unless car wash

**§C — Plumbing Fixtures** — Verify fixture schedule + min fixture count calc + accessibility path.

**§D — Sanitary/Vent System** — Largest deficiency volume. Verify each line on P2.x sheets + riser diagram. Look for missing cleanouts, undersized vents, missing trap seal protection, wrong slope.

**§E — Water Supply System** — Verify water-side schedules + backflow preventer schedule. Hot-water recirculation required for buildings > 50' run from heater to outlet (per §607.2).

**§F — Storm + Non-Potable** — Verify on P4.x. Mark each line; mark whole non-potable line **N/A** if no greywater/rainwater system: "No non-potable water reuse system in scope."

**§G — Interceptors + Separators** — Mark **N/A for whole section** unless trigger:
- Restaurant/food service → grease interceptor (Complies)
- Auto repair/garage → oil/sand interceptor
- Otherwise → "No interceptors in project scope."

**§H — Fuel-Gas + Medical Gas Systems** — Covered in `07c`.

**§I — Accessibility for Plumbing Fixtures** — Verify ANSI A117.1 compliance on toilet rooms.

## Comments column policy (Plumbing-specific)

- ✅ "OK" or blank for Complies
- ✅ N/A scope reason — e.g., "No commercial kitchen; no grease interceptor required"
- ✅ "See Deficiency Log #X"
- ❌ Suggested fixes / design opinions
