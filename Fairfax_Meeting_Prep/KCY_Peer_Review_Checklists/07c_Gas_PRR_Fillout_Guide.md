## Fill-rules

| Mark | When | Comment |
|------|------|---------|
| ☑ Complies | Drawings + sizing calc + valve schedule show compliance | Blank or "OK" |
| ☐ N/A | All-electric building OR no medical gas | One-line scope reason |
| ☐ + comment | Deficiency | "See Deficiency Log #X" |

## §H — Fuel-Gas and Medical Gas Systems

### Fuel-Gas lines (top half of §H)

For each line, verify on gas piping plan + riser + sizing calc:

| PRR line | Verify |
|----------|--------|
| Piping materials + sizing per VFGC §402 + §403 | Material schedule + sizing calc |
| Piping locations per VFGC §404 | Plan view (not in inaccessible cavity, not in supply duct, etc.) |
| **Shutoff valve at each appliance, identified per VFGC §409** | Plan + valve schedule (most-cited deficiency for fuel-gas) |
| **Underground plastic piping with tracer wire or approved equivalent per VFGC §404.17.3** | UG piping detail (most-cited deficiency for UG installations) |

### Medical Gas lines (bottom half of §H — N/A for non-healthcare projects)

Mark **whole medical-gas sub-block N/A** if no medical gas: "Project does not include medical gas; non-healthcare scope."

If healthcare:
| PRR line | Verify |
|----------|--------|
| Med gas pipe + fittings per NFPA 99 §5.1.10.1 | Material schedule |
| System design + construction per NFPA 99 §5.1.3.3.2 | Sealed RDP design + calcs |
| Zone valve locations per NFPA 99 §5.1.4.6 | Plan + valve schedule |
| Shutoff valve labeling per NFPA 99 §5.1.11.2 | Specification + tag schedule |

## Comments column policy (Gas-specific)

- ✅ "OK" or blank for Complies
- ✅ N/A scope reason — "All-electric scope; no fuel gas" / "No medical gas; commercial office occupancy"
- ✅ "See Deficiency Log #X"
- ❌ Suggested fixes / pipe-sizing recommendations
