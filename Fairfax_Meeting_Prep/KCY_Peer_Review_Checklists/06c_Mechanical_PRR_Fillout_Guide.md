## Fill-rules (recap)

| Mark | When | Comment |
|------|------|---------|
| ☑ Complies | Drawings + schedules show compliance | Blank or "OK" |
| ☐ N/A | Doesn't apply (e.g., no kitchen, no parking garage) | One-line scope reason |
| ☐ + comment | Deficiency | "See Deficiency Log #X" |

## Section-by-section

**§A — General Building Information** — Verify each line on M0.0 + roof plan + equipment schedules. **A2 (UL design # / prescriptive method for fire-rated components)** — coordinate with arch wall ratings. **A4 (roof plan with all RTU equipment)** — both existing AND new must be shown for tenant scope when units are touched.

**§B — Ventilation** — Largest deficiency volume + most common rejection. Verify:
- Min OSA calc table per zone exists on plans (most-cited deficiency)
- OSA delivered to unit (CFM) ≥ OSA calculated (CFM) — fill the blanks on the form line
- Smoke detector in return upstream of OSA when shared > 2,000 CFM (§606.2.1)

**§C — Exhaust** — Most lines N/A unless trigger:
- Parking garage → applies if scope includes garage
- Battery system → N/A unless EV/UPS/data battery room
- Medical gas storage → N/A unless healthcare
- Repair garage → N/A unless auto repair
- Manicure/pedicure → N/A unless salon
- Toilet exhaust → almost always Complies (50/75 cfm per fixture)

### Clothes dryers + Commercial Kitchen sub-sections
Mark **whole sub-section N/A** if no dryer or no kitchen in scope. Otherwise verify each line.

**§D — Combustion Air** — One line. Mark N/A if all-electric building: "All-electric; no fuel-burning appliances."

**§E — Ducts + Dampers** — Critical for FP cross-check:
- E1 (corridors NOT serving duct) — verify on M2.x + life safety plan
- E6 (ductwork in rated assemblies conforms to listed assembly) — coordinate with arch fire-rated assembly schedule
- E7 (multi-floor duct penetration protection) — verify shaft details + dampers

### Fire dampers / Smoke dampers
Critical — cross-check with FP review (`02b §F25` smoke + fire damper locations). Fairfax frequently cites missing dampers at rated wall penetrations. Verify damper schedule + plan locations match arch rated walls.

### Smoke control connection
If §909 smoke control system in project → "Connection to smoke removal system" must be on mechanical drawings. Cross-ref `02b §F9` for smoke control submittal pipeline.

## Comments column policy (Mechanical-specific)

- ✅ "OK" or blank for Complies
- ✅ N/A scope reason — e.g., "No commercial kitchen in scope" / "No parking garage"
- ✅ "See Deficiency Log #X"
- ❌ Suggested fixes / sizing recommendations / sequence-of-operation rewrites
