# Mount Pleasant, SC — Third-Party Inspection / Plan Review

**AHJ:** Town of Mount Pleasant Building Inspection Division
**Verified:** 2026-04-27

---

## 1. Overview

The Town of Mount Pleasant explicitly allows third-party inspections **on a per-project, prior-approval basis**. There is **no published pre-qualification application or formal TPI program registry**. Each project requires a meeting with the Building Inspection Division to gain prior approval.

**Per Mount Pleasant Building Inspection page (verified 2026-04-27):**
> "Some projects may be able to use the option for an approved 3rd Party to perform the majority of your inspections, however you must gain prior approval first... For more information, please contact us to schedule your meeting today."

**On TPPR (Third-Party Plan Review):**
> "The Town offers the option for 3rd Party Plan Review for New Commercial Construction projects only when plan review lead-times may be delayed due to high volume, but currently their lead-time is 4-6 weeks, so Third Party Plan Review is not available at this time."

**Bottom line for KCY:**
- TPI: Available on a project-by-project basis, gated by Building Inspection Division meeting + approval. Lower bar than Charleston (no formal pre-qualification packet) but higher friction (one meeting per project).
- TPPR: Currently CLOSED. Only opens when city plan-review lead time exceeds threshold. Worth periodic check-ins (quarterly) to monitor.

**Status:** NEEDS_OUTREACH — schedule a discovery meeting with Building Inspection Division to (a) understand approval criteria and (b) get on a list for the next time TPPR opens.

## 8. Submission Process

1. Email or phone Building Inspection Division to request a TPI discovery meeting.
2. Attend meeting; present credentials + insurance + project scope.
3. If approved, perform inspections per project and file reports per agreed format via OPAL portal.
4. For TPPR: monitor town's plan-review lead time; pitch when it exceeds 4–6 weeks (currently below threshold = TPPR closed).
