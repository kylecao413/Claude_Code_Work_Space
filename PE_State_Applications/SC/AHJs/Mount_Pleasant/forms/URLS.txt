Town of Mount Pleasant, SC — No formal TPI/SI registration forms
Verified 2026-04-27.

Mount Pleasant does NOT publish a TPI pre-qualification packet. Approval is per-project,
gated by a Building Inspection Division discovery meeting (email/phone first).
