# Greenville, SC — Third-Party Inspection / Special Inspector

**AHJ:** City of Greenville Building & Permit Center
**Adjacent AHJ:** Greenville **County** Building Safety (NOT same as City — but governs all unincorporated Greenville County, which is most of the metro construction volume)
**Verified:** 2026-04-27

---

## 1. Overview

**Two distinct AHJs in the Greenville metro:**

1. **City of Greenville** (within city limits) — Building & Permit Center. **No formal TPI / SI program published on city site.** Special inspections handled per SC LLR statewide framework + IBC Ch. 17.

2. **Greenville County** (unincorporated areas + adjacent municipalities) — **publishes a formal Special Inspection Procedure document** (last revised June 2023). This is the more important AHJ for KCY because of the construction volume.

**Per Greenville County procedure document (verified 2026-04-27):**
> "Any individual offering to perform special inspections or testing shall submit a registration or license issued by the South Carolina Building Codes Council, the South Carolina Board of Architectural Examiners or the South Carolina Board of Professional Engineers and Land Surveyors, for approval by the building official."

> "Special inspectors shall be hired directly by the owner, as the contractor is forbidden to do so by code. Special-inspection inspections do not take the place of the required SCBC Chapter 1 inspections that are normally performed by Greenville County Building Inspectors (or approved third party inspectors)."

**Bottom line for KCY:** Greenville County recognizes (a) SC LLR SI registration, (b) SC RA license, OR (c) SC PE license. After SC PE comity issues, KCY's Yue Cao can submit credentials to the County Building Official for approval. **County is more formalized than City and accepts third-party Ch. 1 inspectors as well as SI.**

## 3. Eligibility

**Greenville County (formal):** must hold ONE of the following + Building Official approval:
- SC LLR Special Inspector registration, OR
- SC Board of Architectural Examiners license (RA), OR
- SC Board of Professional Engineers and Land Surveyors license (PE)

KCY route once SC PE comity issues: present SC PE license to County Building Official for approval per category.

**City of Greenville:** Per public-facing pages, no separate pre-qualification published. Same SC LLR framework presumed to apply.

## 8. Submission Process

### Greenville County
1. SI agency submits SC LLR / SC PE / SC RA credential to County Building Official for approval.
2. Owner engages approved SI agency for project (contractor cannot hire SI per code).
3. SI files inspection reports per project; final report at project closeout.

### City of Greenville
Use city Permit Center portal; SI scope follows IBC Ch. 17 as adopted in SC.
