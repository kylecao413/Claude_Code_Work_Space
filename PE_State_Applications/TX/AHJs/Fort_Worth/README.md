# Fort Worth, TX — Third-Party Plan Review / Inspection (TPR/TPI)

**Verified:** 2026-04-27
**Compiled by:** Claude Code, BCC Business Automation

---

## 1. Overview

Fort Worth operates the most mature **Certified Third-Party Firms** program of the major TX cities — predates HB 14 (88R, 2023) by years. Run by the Development Services Department (DSD). Firms meeting minimum education + experience standards are added to the published Certified Firms list and can perform plan review and inspection work directly for permit applicants. HB 14 statewide 15-day backstop also applies.

Most recent published list: **August 2023** (PDF). Need to confirm whether DSD has issued a 2025/2026 update.

Notable certified firms include Metro Code, LLC and Bureau Veritas — both ICC-cert dominant, similar BCC profile.

## 3. Eligibility

Per published list intro: "firms that have met minimum standards of education and experience established by the City." Specifically:
- Verified TX PE/RA on staff OR ICC certifications matching trade(s)
- Resume/CV showing relevant TPR/TPI experience
- Discipline-specific (Building / Mechanical / Electrical / Plumbing / Fire Protection)

**BCC fit:** Strong — Yue's TX PE in flight + ICC MCP covers all 5 trades + several existing TPI projects in DC/MD/VA.

## 8. Submission Process

- Email DSD requesting Certified Third-Party Firm application packet
- Submit application + credentials
- DSD review + approval → addition to next published Certified Firms list
- Per project: applicant submits via Fort Worth EDR system; certified firm performs review/inspection; signed report returned
