# Plano, TX — Third-Party Plan Review / Inspection (TPR/TPI)

**Verified:** 2026-04-27
**Compiled by:** Claude Code, BCC Business Automation

---

## 1. Overview

Plano runs a formal **Expedited Third-Party Building Plans Review** program through Plano Building Inspection Services (BIS). Applicants choose a **Third Party Organization (TPO)** from the City's published approved list, who performs the review on a fee paid directly to the TPO (in addition to City permit fees). Plano BIS retains final approval authority.

This is a clear public registration program. Bureau Veritas (Plano office) and True North Compliance Services are among the active TPOs. HB 14 (88R, 2023) statewide backstop also applies as fallback.

## 3. Eligibility

Per Plano TPO Plan Review process docs:
- TPO firm must be on the Plano BIS approved TPO list
- Application requires firm credentials, ICC certs, TX PE/RA on staff, insurance
- Discipline-specific (Building, Mech, Elec, Plumb, Energy)

## 8. Submission Process

- Apply to Plano BIS using TPO Plan Review Services Application
- Approval → addition to BIS approved TPO list
- Per-project: applicant selects approved TPO from City list when filing permit; TPO performs review; signed report submitted to BIS for final approval
