# Houston, TX — Third-Party Plan Review / Inspection (TPR/TPI)

**Verified:** 2026-04-27
**Compiled by:** Claude Code, BCC Business Automation

---

## 1. Overview

Houston operates the **Expedited Review Service (ERS)** under §1.18.1.8 of the Houston Building Code. ERS is a city-administered fast-track program where reviews are performed by **Bureau Veritas** (under contract approved by Houston City Council in March 2022) within 24 business hours — NOT a public list of independent third-party reviewers. This is the dominant Houston path; HB 14 (88R, 2023) statewide 15-day backstop also applies as a separate fallback.

The Houston Building Code Enforcement (BCE) division within the Houston Permitting Center (HPC) maintains:
- **Approved Agencies** for Special Inspections (Ch. 17 IBC) — published list, separate from ERS
- **Approved Fabricators** — published list

There is **no public "approved third-party plan reviewer firms" list** beyond the BV contract. Independent firms hoping to do TPR work in Houston need to (a) become a BV subcontractor, (b) work via HB 14 backstop on individual projects, or (c) pursue Special Inspection Agency listing.

## 5. Fees

ERS fees (paid by applicant to City for the BV-performed review):
- **Minimum (4 hrs):** $292.97 per trade + $30.51 admin fee
- **Beyond 4 hrs:** $76.28 per hour + $30.51 admin fee per hour

These are city-charged fees, not paid to BCC. If BCC works HB 14 backstop directly with applicant, BCC sets its own fee.

## 7. Plan Review Report Format

No published Houston-specific TPR template. HB 14 only requires written certification of code compliance. ERRC (Emergency Responder Radio Coverage) inspection reports submitted to `HPC-RA@houstontx.gov` per the published Building Code Enforcement guideline — that is the only published TPR-style report path BCE uses.

## 8. Submission Process

- ERS: Email request between **6:00–8:00 AM** (one project per email; first 30 daily). Pay via iPermits or in person at HPC.
- HB 14: No defined city portal; BCC submits directly to BCE per project after AHJ misses 15-day deadline.
