## Overview

City of Miami Building Department maintains its own Private Provider program separate from Miami-Dade County. **City of Miami restricts PP scope**: PPs may NOT do plans review only — they must do inspections (or both review + inspection). PP-permitted disciplines: Building, Roofing, Structural, Electrical, Mechanical, Plumbing. NOT permitted: Zoning, Fire, Public Works (handled by City).

## Eligibility

Per FL §553.791:
- FL-licensed PE (Ch. 471)
- OR FL Architect (Ch. 481)
- OR FL BCA (Part XII, Ch. 468)
- "Standard certificate" holders restricted to residential ≤1,000 sq ft

## Insurance Requirements

Per FL §553.791(18): $1M Pro Liab statutory minimum. City of Miami requires Certificates of Insurance + 5-year tail coverage on claims-made (per §553.791(16)). VERIFY whether City requires $2M.
