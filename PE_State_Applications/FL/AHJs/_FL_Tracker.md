## Cross-AHJ Form Compatibility Notes

- **Notice to Building Official Affidavit** is statewide-templated under §553.791 — most AHJs accept the standard format, but some (Cape Coral, Charlotte) provide their own. Use AHJ-specific form when available.
- **DAR (Duly Authorized Representative) Affidavit** is also statewide-templated.
- **Plan Compliance Affidavit** is more variable — Pompano, Charlotte, Cape Coral, Miami-Dade, Tampa all have specific forms.
- **Inspection Report templates** are most variable — Cape Coral and likely Miami-Dade prescribe specific formats.
