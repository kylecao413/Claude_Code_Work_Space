## Overview

City of Pompano Beach (Broward County) operates a Private Provider program. Pompano Beach is one of the few Broward cities with a public PP page (Broward operates city-by-city — see § 2.1 research). Audits performed per published SOP.

## Eligibility

Per FL §553.791:
- FL-licensed architects or engineers
- BCAIB-certified plans examiners or inspectors
- Approved Duly Authorized Representatives (DARs)
- Cannot inspect work the firm designed or constructed (statutory conflict of interest)
