# City of Miami Beach, FL — Private Provider Registration

**Last Verified:** 2026-04-27
**Status:** READY (program documented; insurance tiers explicit; contact email captured)

## Overview

City of Miami Beach operates a Private Provider program with **explicit tiered insurance requirements** based on project value. Pre-construction meeting required for projects exceeding $1M. Coastal flood-zone projects in Special Flood Hazard Areas have additional documentation requirements (per general FL coastal AHJ practice; specifics not in WebFetch result).

## Eligibility

Per FL §553.791:
- FL-licensed PE (Ch. 471)
- OR FL Architect (Ch. 481)
- OR FL Standard Certificate holders for residential alterations ≤1,000 sq ft

## Insurance Requirements (TIERED — Miami Beach specific)

- **Projects ≤ $5M construction cost**: $1M per occurrence / $2M aggregate
- **Projects > $5M construction cost**: $2M per occurrence / $4M aggregate
- Claims-made coverage requires 5 years of tail coverage post-services
- Insurer A.M. Best rating: minimum **A**
- Certificate required BEFORE performing services

Higher than statutory minimum — Miami Beach requires more coverage than most FL AHJs.
