# Florida — FBPE

## Submission
**Paper only.** FBPE does not accept electronic applications. Print, sign, attach check, mail.

## Files in this folder
- `FL_PE_Application.pdf` — blank reference copy
- `FL_PE_Application_PREFILLED.pdf` — ready to print; fill TBD markers by hand before mailing

## Fee
**$230** — personal check, business check, cashier's check, or money order payable to **FBPE**. No credit cards. No online payment.

Breakdown:
- $130 non-refundable application fee
- $100 initial license fee

## What to include in the envelope
1. Application pages 1-5 (completed, signed, dated)
2. $230 check to FBPE
3. Copy of government photo ID (driver's license) **+ copy of Permanent Resident Card** (required as proof of legal presence)
4. Do NOT include pages 6-11 (supplemental verification / reference forms) — NCEES Record covers those

## NCEES transmittal
Order NCEES Record transmittal to **Florida Board of Professional Engineers** separately via your NCEES account. $175 first state, $100 each additional.

## Notary
**Not required** on comity track with NCEES Record.

## Before you sign
Fill these TBD fields on the prefilled PDF by hand (or update Master_Info.yaml and regenerate):
- Education: BS grad date from Dalian (MM/YYYY)
- Exams: FE / PE Electrical / PE Civil / PE Fire exam dates + locations
- VA PE Civil-Geotechnical license # (if separate from 0402056707)
- Verifying engineers' license numbers (for employment rows beyond DEI)
- Reference PEs #2 and #3 (name, PE #, address)

## Processing
~4-8 weeks after mail receipt. Status notice within 30 days.
