# North Carolina — NCBELS

## Submission
**Online short form only.** With NCEES Record (Option 1), no PDF needed.

## Files in this folder
- `NC_Portal_Answers.md` — cheat sheet — copy/paste into each portal field

## Fee
**$0 NC-side** with Option 1 (NCEES Record). Fee paid entirely to NCEES for transmittal.

This is the **cheapest and fastest** of the six states.

## Portal URL
https://www.ncbels.org/application/initial-comity-pe-licensure/

Choose: **"Option 1 — Comity with NCEES Record"**.

## What to include
With NCEES Record, nothing else required:
- No SERs
- No transcripts
- No references
- No NC-side fee

Just:
1. Complete short online form (use `NC_Portal_Answers.md`)
2. Upload copy of Permanent Resident Card
3. NCEES Record transmittal (handled via your NCEES account)

## NCEES transmittal
Order transmittal to **NC Board of Examiners for Engineers and Surveyors**.

## Notary
Not required on Option-1 short form.

## Processing
~8 weeks typical; faster with Record already on file.

## Tracking
Portal dashboard.
