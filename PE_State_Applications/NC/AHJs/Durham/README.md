# City of Durham / Durham County — City-County Building & Safety Department

**AHJ:** Durham City-County Building & Safety Department (single department covers both City of Durham and Durham County)
**Program type:** **No formal third-party program identified.** SI handled per NCBC Ch. 17 with NC PE/RA acceptance per project.
**Verified:** 2026-04-27
**Status for KCY Engineer PLLC:** NEEDS_OUTREACH (Durham has no published TPI form/registration — KCY must phone/email to confirm process)

---

## 1. Statutory & Code Basis

- **NCGS Ch. 160D Article 11** + **NCBC Chapter 17**.
- Durham follows the NC State Building Code; SI is the NC PE/RA's responsibility per project.

## 2. Program Overview

The City-County Building & Safety Department provides permit, plan review, and inspection services for both the City and Durham County. **No public third-party inspection program page exists** as of 2026-04-27 — neither in their main inspections workflow nor under the contractor resources section.

The available public docs cover:
- Schedule routine inspections via the LDO portal (login required, must be permit applicant)
- Informal Internal Review process (PDF on doc-center)
- Approval Processes & Inspections (general workflow page)

This is a "soft market" — work happens via per-project NC PE/RA acceptance under Ch. 17, but Durham has no website-published agreement form.
