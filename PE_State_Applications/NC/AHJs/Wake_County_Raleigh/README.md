## 1. Statutory & Code Basis

- **NCGS Ch. 160D Article 11** — owner-elected TPI/TPPR.
- **NCBC Chapter 17 (revised Section 1704)** — special inspections on non-residential / commercial projects must be performed by a NC-registered design professional or an inspector under their responsible charge.

## 2. Program Overview

Raleigh has **no formal annual registration program**. Instead, a **Third Party Inspections Agreement** is filed **per project** before construction starts. A pre-construction meeting may be required for any project that falls into the SI category. Wake County (for unincorporated areas + the towns of Knightdale/Rolesville/Wendell/Zebulon) operates similarly — agreement-based, no standing list.

## 4. Eligibility

- Must be **NC-registered design professional** (PE or RA) — or an inspector working under their responsible charge.
- No state-level cert beyond NC PE/RA required for the agreement signer.
- ICC certifications recommended for individual field inspectors but not strictly required by Raleigh's program text.

## 5. Insurance Requirements

- **Not specified** in the program documentation. Raleigh historically asks for COI evidence at pre-construction meeting; figure not published.
- **OPEN QUESTION (Q1).**

## 6. Fees

- **No registration fee.** No annual renewal. Per-project agreement only.
- Standard building-permit fees apply per project.

## 8. Plan Review / Statement / Final Report Format

- **Statement of Special Inspections** — Raleigh accepts the NC State Construction Office template (SCO Feb 2020 rev) or any equivalent that lists SI categories per NCBC Ch. 17.
- **Final Report format** — not published as a Raleigh-specific PDF; per practice, the NC PE/RA submits a sealed letter summarizing each Schedule line item with pass/fail/observation findings.

## 9. Submission Process

1. Owner/GC selects KCY Engineer PLLC (with NC-licensed PE) as the third-party.
2. Schedule of Special Inspection Services + Statement uploaded with permit application.
3. Third Party Inspections Agreement signed by GC, owner, KCY's NC PE, and the City; filed before construction.
4. Pre-construction meeting held (Jim Thompson coordinates).
5. KCY field inspectors perform Ch. 17 inspections; reports filed per project.
6. At completion, NC PE submits sealed Final Report letter; CO/CC released.
