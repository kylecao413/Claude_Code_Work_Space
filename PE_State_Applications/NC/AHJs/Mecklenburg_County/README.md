## 1. Statutory & Code Basis

- **NCGS Ch. 160D Article 11** — owner-elected third-party plan review and inspection. Reviewer/inspector must be NC-registered architect or engineer (or under their responsible charge).
- **NCBC Chapter 17** — Special Inspections (the trigger; copies the IBC Ch. 17 framework with NC amendments).
- **NCSBC Chapter 17 + 2018 NC Administration & Enforcement Code Appendix B** — supplemental requirements that Mecklenburg enforces.

## 2. Program Overview

- Launched 2006-01-17 as `meck-si.com` — paperless special-inspection management portal (project field reports, qualifications, fees, document retrieval).
- **As of 2025–2026, the program is migrating to Accela.** Code Enforcement announced "Meck-SI Moves to Accela" — the new Accela portal becomes the preferred channel for all SI projects starting February (transitional period; legacy meck-si.com still partially live).
- Two registrant types:
  - **SIF** — Special Inspection Firm (registers first; lists firm's address, NC PE/RA in responsible charge, etc.)
  - **DSI / ASI** — Designated Special Inspector / Authorized Special Inspector (individual inspectors registered under a SIF)
- **RDPIRC** — Registered Design Professional in Responsible Charge (the NC PE/RA who signs off final reports).

## 4. Eligibility

- **Firm-level (SIF):** Must designate at least one **NC-registered PE or Architect** as the RDPIRC. Firm must have NC business registration (foreign LLC qualification for KCY Engineer PLLC).
- **Individual-level (DSI/ASI):** Either (a) NC PE/RA, or (b) ICC-certified special inspector in the relevant discipline. ICC disciplines that meck-si recognizes mirror the IBC Ch. 17 framework: Reinforced Concrete (47), Structural Masonry, Structural Steel & Bolting (S1), Structural Welding (S2/AWS CWI), Prestressed Concrete, Spray-Applied Fireproofing, Soils, plus standard Building/Mech/Elec/Plumb code certs for non-structural items.
- For KCY: Yue's NC PE (in flight) + ICC MCP + state-licensed PE in VA/DC/MD will satisfy individual qualification once NC PE issues. PE Fire Protection (passed 2026-04-16) covers FP/sprayed-fireproofing categories.

## 9. Plan Review / Statement / Final Report Format

- **Statement of Special Inspections** — must be placed on the construction drawings adjacent to NC OSFM Appendix B. Mecklenburg's template (form #4 above) is the format the AHJ wants.
- **Schedule of Special Inspections** — accompanying matrix (form #5).
- **Final Report** — DSI completes form #8; RDPIRC seals letter #9. Both filed via Accela (or legacy meck-si).

## 10. Submission Process

1. Firm registers on meck-si.com / Accela (firm-level SIF profile + RDPIRC PE seal).
2. Each inspector registers individually under firm (DSI/ASI) with cert uploads + $225 fee.
3. Per project: GC/owner names firm in permit application; firm uploads Statement + Schedule; pre-construction meeting held.
4. During construction: DSIs submit field reports through portal.
5. At completion: DSI signs Final Report; RDPIRC signs RDPIRC Letter; final filed via portal → CO released.
